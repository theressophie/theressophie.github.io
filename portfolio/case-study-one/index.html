Digital Pharmacy

Industry

Healthcare

Service

Research, Design, User Tests

Duration

6 months (2020)

Challenge

The current process of ordering your medications for the first time is really complicated.

Project Goal

Create the easiest way to submit a prescription for chronically ill people with permanent medication.

Process

Team

We worked together in an interdisciplinary team with one Business Design Architect (BDA), Service Designer, UX Architect (me) and Strategist on our site and a Product Owner, Business Analyst and Customer Experience Manager on the client’s site. The BDA lead through the process whereas the Service Designer orchestrated touchpoints, places, processes and people, which together support the full user experience and the Strategist developed the business strategy. My role as an UX Architect was to ensure that the designs matches the needs and capabilities of the people for whom they are intended.

Solution

Research

Benchmark Analysis & Market Research

…to get info about competitors, stakeholders, clients & products in the healthcare sector.

Qualitative Interviews

…with patients, caretakers and experts in healthcare industry

Personas

Users, in the form of user group profiles and personas define one part of the context of use description.

Key Insights

In the context of use we identified patterns that we grouped into key insights. These artefacts provide the guiding principles for future outputs in the project.

User Journey

Design Solutions

Wireframes

Wireframes are a useful tool for discussing concepts with the customer. Wireframes serve as a first visualisation of the concept to make sure that you are on the same page with your customer‘s ideas and needs.

Another important point is that you can iterate different versions of solutions quickly and easily without losing much time and refine these throughout the process.

Screen Designs

Wireframes are the foundation for screen designs and display the user‘s interactions with the application. Whereas screen designs represent the look and feel.

As a starting point the screen designs focused on two main parts of the current application that needed refinement: onboarding and ordering.

Iteration & Improvement

Click Dummy

We added interaction to the screen designs to create a non-functional click dummy. A non-functional click dummy provides an easy possibility to test interactions with real users.

Test Plan & Test Script

The test plan defines the scope and the goal of the test. The test script describes the procedure of the test and guides the user through the process along predefined tasks.

User Tests

The test supervisor guided the participant through the onboarding process along the test plan. A silent observer was taking notes.

Synthesis

Before we did the tests we created hypotheses. In the next step we analysed how many testers proved or disproved those hypotheses. Additionally we assigned a severity level to each issue mentioned by our test users. For the most severe problems we derived action items. We updated our prototype based on those action items and validated our update in another test.
